Destination of manang and mustang

Attraction and Activities

  • scenic Landscape

    • : Manang is renowned for its breathtaking landscapes. The region is characterized by towering peaks, lush forests, and pristine rivers. The Annapurna and Gangapurna mountain ranges dominate the skyline, providing a dramatic backdrop for visitors.

  • Annapurna Circuit

    • Manang is a popular stopover for trekkers along the Annapurna Circuit. The trek offers an opportunity to experience diverse landscapes, from subtropical forests to alpine meadows, and provides a sense of accomplishment as you reach high-altitude destinations like Thorong La Pass

  • Adventure and trekkinng

    • Mustang offers fantastic trekking opportunities, including the Upper Mustang trek, which takes you through remote villages and offers breathtaking vistas of the Himalayas. The challenging terrain and unique culture make it an adventure seeker's paradise
